Research Interests¶

Primary Research¶

  • Plasma interaction with spacecraft bodies and Langmuir probes in the polar ionosphere.

  • Ion dynamics and sheath structure in Inertial Electrostatic Confinement Fusion machines.

  • Magnetized Plasma Sheaths.

  • Dust charging in laboratory and astrophysical plasmas.

Numerical Tools/Codes¶

  • Kinetic

    • XOOPIC (X11-based Object Oriented Particle in Cell code), Michigan State University, USA.

    • PINC (Particle-In-Cell code), University of Oslo, Norway.

    • PICSP (Particle-in-Cell Simulation of Plasma), Centre of Plasma Physics, Institute for Plasma Research, India.

    • Gkeyll Vlasov-Maxwell code, Princeton Plasma Physics Laboratory (PPPL), USA.

  • Fluid

    • IDFM - (Ion Dynamics using Fluid Modeling), Centre of Plasma Physics, Institute for Plasma Research, India.

Apart from above, I am also a Python enthusiast and I try to use this language to develop visualization toolkits and basic machine learning models.
